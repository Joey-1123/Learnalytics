About Learnalytics

Learnalytics is designed to simplify student management by providing powerful insights, performance tracking, and data-driven decisions.

Our Mission

Our goal is to make student data easy to manage and understand, helping educators focus more on teaching and less on manual work.

Key Features

Student Tracking

Monitor attendance, marks, and overall performance.

Analytics

Get insights into student progress and trends.

Reports

Generate clear and useful performance reports.

AI Insights

Smart recommendations based on student data.
